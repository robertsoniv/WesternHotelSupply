Shipping

Free Shipping

All orders of $250 or more of merchandise qualify for free standard, ground shipping. This offer only applies to orders shipping within the contiguous United States. Certain weight and size restrictions may apply. Qualifying orders must ship to the same location to qualify for free shipping.

Ground Orders

For delivery time, standard ground shipping normally requires 3-7 business days for delivery. We ship all ground orders in the continental United States by UPS Ground, Fed Ex Ground, Spee-Dee Delivery, or U.S. Postal Service Parcel Post. We select the best possible shipper based on your location and which warehouse it will ship from so you can get your item in the fastest possible way. To view rates select Ground as a shipping option in the shopping cart at the time of checkout or contact our Customer Service department.

Please provide a physical street address for all deliveries. At this time we cannot ship to PO Boxes.

Expedited Orders

You can get your order faster by upgrading to Expedited Shipping. Orders will generally ship out by the lead time listed above. If your Expedited order will take longer, you will be notified via email or a telephone call. Some products cannot be shipped expedited. We ship all expedited shipping orders via UPS, Fed Ex, Spee-Dee Delivery or U.S. Postal Service Priority & Express Mail. We select the best possible shipper based on your location and which warehouse it will ship from so you can get your item in the fastest way. To receive expedited shipping, just select 3 Day, 2 Day or 1 Day Shipping when checking out online or ask for it when talking to a Customer Service Agent. To determine expedited shipping rates: Simply add the item to the shopping cart and select 3-Day, 2-Day or 1-Day shipping. Please provide both a phone number and a valid email address for expedited shipping. Please note this policy does not apply to orders shipped to Alaska or Hawaii.

Canadian, Alaskan, and Hawaiian Orders

We love our friends outside the lower 48 in the US but we apologize that we cannot offer free shipping to these locations. Canadian and all other foreign customers are responsible for all duties, taxes, customs and any other fees related to your order. We reserve the right to refuse transactions in violation of current Free Trade Agreements.

Additional Handling Fees

We do not charge additional handling fees on any order.

Returns

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our Customer Service Representatives will do their best to ensure you are getting the products that meet your needs. Changed your mind? No problem! If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return any item* within 30 days of delivery of your merchandise.

*Please Note: Merchandise that has been used or damaged is no longer in new and ready-to-sell condition and should not be returned and cannot be refunded, replaced or exchanged unless it has a manufacturer defect or was damaged in shipping. All returned merchandise must be in its original packaging, including all parts and paperwork. Customized, personalized and made to order products are not returnable for any reason besides a manufacturing defect.

Our Customer Service Department is available to help you return merchandise Monday - Thursday, 7am to 6pm CST and Friday 8am-5pm CST at 1-800-645-3856. You can also process your return anytime by completing our Online Merchandise Return Request Form

Custom/ Made-to-Order Products:

Customize, personalized and made to order products are not returnable for any reason besides a manufacturing defect.

Returning items? Just follow these instructions:

Notify us by completing an Online Merchandise Return Request Form or contacting our friendly Customer Service Department.

You will be issued a Return Authorization Number (RA#) and provided with return instructions on what to do next in accordance with our Return Policy within 24-48 hours upon receipt of your Return Merchandise Request. (Excludes: Saturdays, Sundays and Holidays).

Properly package your return merchandise to avoid damages. Please include all original packaging, parts and paperwork.

Why Do I Need an RA Number?

This is necessary for:

1. Identification of your returned merchandise. Our warehouses receive hundreds of packages every day. Without an RA number (Return Authorization Number) identifying it, your package may become lost.

2. Returning your package to the right warehouse. We have multiple warehouses throughout the United States that we ship from so you receive your order as fast as possible. Returned merchandise must be sent back to the proper location. Shipping a return to the wrong warehouse or our mailing address will significantly delay the processing of your return and may result in additional fees or the loss of your package. We are not responsible for lost packages.

3. Avoiding lost returns. Without a proper RA number and Tracking number, your package can easily get lost; identifying a lost package is almost impossible. If we do not receive or cannot find your package, we cannot process your return.

My Order Was Damaged in Shipping

If your order arrives with obvious damage and you refuse the shipment, please notify us immediately by contacting our Customer Service Department. We will make arrangements to ship a replacement as soon as possible. If you discover that your order was damaged after receiving it, please complete an Online Merchandise Return Request Form. You will be issued a Case ID Number (CR#) and we will provide you with instructions on what to do next in accordance with our return policy. Arrangements to ship a replacement will be made as soon as possible. In the meantime, please prepare the damaged merchandise for pickup. In most cases, the carrier that shipped the merchandise (usually UPS/FedEx) will be by to pick it up for inspection. Please keep a copy of the pickup tracking number, should you need it for future reference. This is your proof that the carrier picked up the merchandise for shipping damage inspection.

Please Note: Providing photos of the damaged box and item will help speed up the claim process tremendously.

My Order Was Lost in Transit

In the event that you do not receive your merchandise because it has been lost in transit, please notify us immediately by contacting our Customer Service Department. We will work with the carrier to find and deliver your order. In most cases, the carrier (usually UPS/FedEx) will find and deliver your package in 1-2 business days. Of course, we will make arrangements to ship a replacement when needed.

There Was an Error in My Order

Although it rarely happens, in the case your order arrives with an error (i.e., what you received is not what you ordered) or is defective, please notify us by completing an Online Merchandise Return Request Form. You will be issued a Case ID Number (CR#) and given instructions for what to do next.

Refunds/Replacements

1. If you are returning your merchandise and would like a refund, your credit card or account will be refunded the original purchase price (plus sales tax if applicable) once the returned merchandise is received back in our warehouse.

2. If you are returning your merchandise and would like a replacement, we will begin processing your replacement order right away; we will process your return once the package is received.

3. Refunds may take an additional 5-7 business days to post to your account; time required varies by financial institution.
